Pizza

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish that consists of a yeasted flatbread typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients baked at a high temperature. It's versatile and perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 packet (7g) dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 300ml warm water
  • 200ml tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Various toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (482°F).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar.
  3. In a separate bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour mixture and stir until a dough forms.
  5. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, until it is smooth and elastic.
  6. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  7. After the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two equal portions.
  8. Roll out each portion on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  9. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  10. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  11. Sprinkle the grated mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  12. Add your desired toppings.
  13. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  14. Remove the pizza from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  15. Slice and serve immediately. Enjoy!
