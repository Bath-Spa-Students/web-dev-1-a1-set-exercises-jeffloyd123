The Good Doctor

The Good Doctor is a medical drama television series that follows Dr. Shaun Murphy, a young surgical resident with autism and savant syndrome, as he navigates the challenges of working at a prestigious hospital while dealing with personal and professional hurdles.

Key Information

  • Genre: Drama
  • Created by: David Shore
  • First aired: September 25, 2017
  • Network: ABC

Cast & Crew

  • Freddie Highmore as Dr. Shaun Murphy
  • Antonia Thomas as Dr. Claire Browne
  • Nicholas Gonzalez as Dr. Neil Melendez
  • Hill Harper as Dr. Marcus Andrews
  • Richard Schiff as Dr. Aaron Glassman
  • Fiona Gubelmann as Dr. Morgan Reznick
  • Christina Chang as Dr. Audrey Lim

Characters

  • Dr. Shaun Murphy - A young surgical resident with autism and savant syndrome.
  • Dr. Claire Browne - A surgical resident and Shaun's friend.
  • Dr. Neil Melendez - A senior surgeon who often challenges Shaun.
  • Dr. Marcus Andrews - A doctor and hospital administrator.
  • Dr. Aaron Glassman - Shaun's mentor and father figure.
  • Dr. Morgan Reznick - A surgical resident with a strong personality.
  • Dr. Audrey Lim - The Chief of Surgery.

Reviews

"The Good Doctor is a touching and thought-provoking series that shines a light on the experiences of individuals with autism. Freddie Highmore delivers a powerful performance that is both captivating and heartfelt." - The Hollywood Reporter

"With its compelling storytelling and strong character development, The Good Doctor has become a standout show in the medical drama genre." - Variety
